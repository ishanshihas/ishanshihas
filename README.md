# 🌊 Where Sunlight ends, curiosity begins. 🌊

## Hi there, my name is Ishan Shihas.

I am an aspiring Marine Biologist based in Qatar who's heading to Canada to study Marine biology. I dive in the Arabian Gulf and turn what I find into data, pairing fieldwork with Python and R to document reef life.

### What I'm working on

**Sealine fish biodiversity** - There is a beach in Qatar called Sealine beach and it is a diving hub filled with artificial coral reefs and wrecks. I am creating a photo backed, GPS verified record of reef fish along this beach's coast. It will be mapped in Python and analysed in R.

### My goals

I hope to build a research focused career in Marine biology, primarily focused on coral reefs and the deep sea.

### What I do

- Field work:  Diving, species ID, logging and mapping different dive sites.
- Python:      Data cleaning, mapping and species image classification.
- R:           Ecological statistics and visualisation.
